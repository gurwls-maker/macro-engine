## 9. External anchor and app policy separation

External anchor:
- says what region has physiological meaning
- does not say the exact app score
- does not create a hard cutoff by itself

App policy coefficient:
- says how fast score should move around the anchor
- needs fixture QA and user confirmation
- must not contradict the external anchor

Examples:

```text
8-12 g/kg carbohydrate:
- external anchor for high training/glycogen context
- not a flat no-penalty upper

Alcohol 112g / 140g:
- external risk region is real
- current score already severe
- next likely issue is copy/product tone, not automatic score tuning

TDEE overload:
- total burn relation is physiologically meaningful
- exact 1.5x/2.0x score severity is app policy

Fat excess:
- fat range and energy density matter
- exact 2.0x/3.0x collapse point is app policy
```
